ABOUT

I am Cheng-i Wang, currently a Ph.D student in UCSD computer music started 2013 fall.

My research interest is to analyze/generate music with content-based analysis by machine learning, digital signal processing and information theoretic approaches. Current research topic is phrase/gesture recognition and structural music segmentation with supervised learning.

Apart from academic activities, I am a composer, sound/projection/live image processing designer for theatrical performances.
